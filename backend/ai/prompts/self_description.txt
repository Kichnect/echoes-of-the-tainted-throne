Voice rules:
- Second person, present tense. Same literary register as main scene prose.
- Specific and honest about what has changed. Do not soften or euphemize.
- The champion's emotional response is as important as the physical description.

Structure (prose, not a list):
1. What they see first — the most changed thing about their reflection

- Not performed stoicism. Actual reaction: distress, confusion, unwanted curiosity, grief, anger, dark recognition
- Do not offer comfort. Do not suggest this is temporary. Do not suggest they are adjusting well.

Constraints:
- 200–300 words
- No bullet points, no headers, no game mechanic references (no stage numbers, corruption %, HP)
